### Section 4: How It Works (800 words)

**H2**: Building Your Soulbook: The Process

**Step 1: Choose Your Life Book** (2 min)
Pick your metaphor - Symphony, Path, or Pillars

**Step 2: Assess Current State** (20-30 min)
For each of the 7 pillars:
- Rate yourself 1-10
- Identify what's working
- Name what's not
- Reflect on past attempts

**Step 3: Create Your Vision** (30-45 min)
For each pillar:
- Describe your 10/10 state
- Paint vivid picture of your ideal
- Identify what becomes possible

**Step 4: Design Your Systems** (30-45 min)
For each pillar:
- Daily rituals that support growth
- Weekly practices
- What to build vs eliminate
- Habit architecture

**Step 5: Set Action Plans** (20-30 min)
For each pillar:
- Top 3 priorities next 30 days
- One micro-commitment this week
- Support/resources needed

**Step 6: Integration & PDF** (15 min)
- Review all 7 pillars together
- See how they connect
- Generate beautiful PDF
- Have your complete Soulbook

**Total Time**: 2-3 hours (can be split across sessions)

**Output**:
- 7 detailed markdown files (4-6 pages each)
- 1 integration document
- 1 beautiful PDF (40-50 pages)
- Your complete life blueprint
